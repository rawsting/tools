Emoji meaning - it's a small digital image or icon used to express an idea or emotion in electronic communication. Created in 1999 in Japan by mister Shigetaka Kurita, emoji were initially used by Japanese mobile operators. In Japanese spelling is 絵文字 , so the symbol 絵 (e) means "picture" and 文字 (moji) means "letter" so it translates to "picture letter".
