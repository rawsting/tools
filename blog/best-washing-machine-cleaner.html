Best Washing Machine Cleaner for sparkling Results​

In this article, we’ll share the top washing machine cleaners that effectively remove build-up and stains for a like-new machine:

 

1. Kitchengenix's Washing Machine Cleaning Tablet

 

Kitchengenix's Washing Machine Cleaning Tablets are specially formulated to eliminate odors and dissolve residue in all parts of your washer. The deodorizing tablets use premium ingredients that get to work instantly, removing even heavy grime and scale build-up.

 

Key features:

  • Removes grime, residue, and bad odors from the drum, pump, filter, pipes, door glass, and gasket

  • EDTA-free advanced formula dissolves tough buildup

  • Eco-friendly tablets with less packaging compared to liquid cleaners

  • Easy to use - just add 1 tablet to an empty wash cycle with hot water

  • Use monthly for fresh, clean laundry loads

2. Surf Excel Washing Descaler Drum

 

Surf Excel Washing Descaler Drum is made specifically for fully automatic washing machines. It removes limescale, grime, and bacteria for a hygienically clean machine.

 

Key features:

  • Dissolves limescale and grime buildupLeaves a fresh lime fragrance

  • Kills bacteria for superior cleaning

  • Safe for both top load and front load metal drums

  • Use monthly by adding to an empty wash cycle

Keep Your Washer Sparkling

Regularly using a dedicated washing machine cleaning product is the best way to remove residue and bacteria for fresh, clean laundry every time. The Kitchengenix Tablets and Surf Excel Descaler Drum are top choices that effectively eliminate build-up and odors.

Follow the directions on the package and use monthly to keep your washer fresh and clean.
